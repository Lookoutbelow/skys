Our Story

Real New York Pizza, Right Here in Daytona

Sky's Pies Pizza is positioned as one of Daytona Beach's local go-to pizza spots, with a current menu that stretches well beyond slices into subs, stromboli, pasta, salads, appetizers, desserts, and family-style combo deals.

The live site emphasizes the range of options available for takeout and curbside pickup, from New York style pizzas in multiple sizes to pasta entrees like baked ziti, lasagna, chicken parmesan, and eggplant rollatini.

It keeps the same neighborhood-pizzeria positioning throughout: straightforward ordering, local service, and a full Italian-American menu designed for everyday lunch and dinner.
