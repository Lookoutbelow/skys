Our Story

Real New York Pizza, Right Here in Daytona

Sky's Pies Pizza brings authentic, thin-crust New York style pizza to Daytona Beach. Every pie is hand-tossed and made fresh, from classic cheese slices to specialty pies loaded with premium toppings.

The menu goes beyond pizza with hot and cold subs, calzones, stromboli, baked pasta, salads, wings, cannoli, tiramisu, and combo deals for families and larger orders.

Whether you need lunch, dinner, pickup, delivery, or catering, Sky's Pies keeps it simple: fresh food, generous portions, and neighborhood reliability.
